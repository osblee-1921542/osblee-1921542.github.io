About Me

I am a professional photographer traveling the world looking to capture memories in the form of a picture. I am interested in taking photos of all kinds of things ranging form people to places to things! Anything!

I travel with my family from place to place meeting new and interesting people. The more I travel, the more I take photos, and the more I take photos, the happier I am. I Love my job and if you are interested in taking a similar path a me, feel free to contact me any time. I frequently update my photo gallery whenever I have the chance. I am planning to go to Spain next!
